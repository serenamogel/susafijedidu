FAQs

What was Ringo Starr’s illness?

Starr was diagnosed with appendicitis at the age of six. He had peritonitis after a routine appendectomy, which sent him into a coma that lasted days.

His treatment took a year, and he spent it in Liverpool’s Myrtle Street Children’s Hospital, away from his family.

Were Ringo and George friends?

In a 2003 interview, Starr stated that when the Beatles split, he remained close friends with George and that the song wonderfully summed up “how I miss him in my heart and in music” for him.

Is Ringo Starr a vegetarian?

Starr has spoken about his diet at several events. Ringo revealed that he is a vegetarian in an interview before his milestone birthday. Every morning, he loves eating broccoli with everything and blueberries, and this is the key to his youthful appearance.
