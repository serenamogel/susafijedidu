Helio Gracie Net Worth

Helio Gracie was a Brazilian martial artist born on October 1, 1913 in Belem, Brazil. He was a co-founder of Gracie Giu-Jitsu and held a 10th degree red belt in Jiu-Jitsu and a 6th degree black belt in Judo. He was a pioneer in the development of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and is widely regarded as one of the most influential martial artists of all time.
