Rowan Blanchard Bio (Wiki)

Who is Rowan Blanchard?

Rowan Blanchard is an American actress.

She is extremely prominent for her role as Riley Matthews on the Disney Channel series “Girl Meets The World.”

She is a hardworking person and has made a respectable position in American T.V and film industry.

Rowan Blanchard: Birth Facts, Family, and Childhood

Rowan was born on October 14, 2001, in Los Angeles, California, U.S.A.

Her nationality is American and belongs to a Mixed (Armenian, Lebanese, Moroccan, Syrian, Swedish, Danish, Norwegian, English, German, Portuguese) ethnicity.

She was born to Elizabeth Blanchard and Mark Blanchard.

She was raised up by her parents in a friendly and entertaining environment along with her siblings named Carmen Blanchard and Shane Blanchard.

Education History

There is no factual information published regarding her educational status.

Rowan Blanchard: Professional Life and Career

Rowan made her professional acting debut in the year 2010 and appeared in the film titled “The Back-up Plan” as a 7-year-old kid.

In the same year, she made her T.V debut and appeared in the T.V film titled “Dance-a-Lot Robot” as ‘Caitlin’ in the main role.

Later in the year 2011, she appeared in the movies Little in Common and Spy Kids: All the Time in the World. 

In 2014, she appeared in a successful T.V series titled “Girl Meets World” as ‘Riley Matthews’.

She was also nominated for ‘The teen Choice Awards’ and ‘Kids’ Choice Awards for the same film.

In 2012, she was nominated for ‘The Young Artist Awards in the category of ‘Best Performance in a Feature Film’.

Besides these, she has also appeared in the television series Best Friends Whenever and Invisible Sister in the year 2015.

Similarly in the year 2017, she appeared in the television series The Goldbergs.
