Career

The talented rapper discovered the then-unknown genre of music when he was just nine years old.

Nas started recording music as a teenager with a neighbor who served as his DJ.

He used to go to the recording studio with rapper and record producer Large Professor in the late 1980s.

Nas frequently produced music in the recording studio, but none of his work was ever officially launched.

Luckily he got a deal to sign to Columbia Records in the early 1990s, and his debut album was released in 1994.

TheSourcee named it the Best Album of 1994, and its success bolstered more Nas hits.

He has kept the spotlight on himself by waging long-running rivalries with fellow rappers such as Jay Z, Young Jeezy, and the late Tupac Shakur.

Nas has had a very successful rap career, with his six albums ranked no. 1 on the Billboard chart.
