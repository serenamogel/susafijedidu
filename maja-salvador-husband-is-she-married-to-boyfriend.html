Who is Maja Salvador Husband? Maja Ross Andres Salvador is a multi-talented Filipino actress, performer, dancer, singer, television host, occasional producer, and talent manager. 

Salvador was born on October 5, 1988. She is also known as the “Majestic Superstar” and “Queen of Revenge Drama” and has dramatically affected the entertainment industry.

She began her career at 14, making her television debut in the series “It Might Be You” in 2003. She gained recognition and a nomination for Best New TV Personality at the 18th PMPC Star Awards for Television.

Her film debut came in 2006 with a supporting role in “Sukob,” which earned her nominations for Best Supporting Actress at the 55th FAMAS Awards and Gawad PASADO Awards.

Throughout her career, Salvador has received critical acclaim and numerous awards for her lead roles in various television series such as “Nagsimula sa Puso,” “Minsan Lang Kita Iibigin,” “Ina, Kapatid, Anak,” “The Legal Wife,” “Bridges of Love,” and “FPJ’s Ang Probinsyano.”

However, her portrayal of Lily Cruz/Ivy Aguas in the top-tier revenge-drama series “Wildflower” cemented her superstardom status.

The show garnered high ratings and earned her nominations and awards from prestigious award-giving bodies.
