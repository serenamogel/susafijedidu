The Meaning Behind The Song: Every Chance I Get by DJ Khaled (featuring Lil Baby &amp; Lil Durk)

Unlocking the Essence of Every Chance I Get by DJ Khaled (featuring Lil Baby & Lil Durk)

Every Chance I Get is one of the hottest tracks on DJ Khaled’s Khaled Khaled album, released on April 30, 2021. The track features two of the hottest rappers in the game right now, Lil Baby and Lil Durk. The song’s message is clear, promote hard work, stay focused and never let anyone or anything come in between you and your goals. Despite the riveting beats and lyrics, the song carries a deeper message, supported by Khaled’s samples that make the song outstanding.

Breaking Down The Intro

The song takes us through Khaled’s journey towards his success and his message that success is achievable for anyone given hard work. The intro is a testament that DJ Khaled is focused on overcoming anything that stands between him and his success. In the intro, Khaled declaratively says the following:

“I do this, every chance I get – ’cause I gotta have it.”

The line summarizes the mentality of achieving success. Hard work is the key to unlocking the potential to achieve your goals.

The Role of Lil Baby in Every Chance I Get

Lil Baby’s verse, which comes in after Khaled’s intro, emphasizes the importance of securing your bag with every opportunity life presents.

“I been savin’ my money, it ain’t no tellin’ what I’m buyin’ (Uh-huh)
My lil’ man a minor, he kept it gangster and took his time (Uh-huh)
All my n**gasgon’eat, no discussion,that’s mandatory (Uh-huh)
Bunch of hollows in theGlock, hope I don’t need it, it’s mandatory (Uh-huh)”

The lines above show Lil Baby’s determination to lead his crew to financial stability, and he emphasizes it by saving his money. Lil Baby is also aware of the dangers that come with the streets and is ready to defend what he has built with his team.

The Role of Lil Durk in Every Chance I Get

Lil Durk’s verses lean more to his code to the streets, where he struggled for his success. He understands that hard work eventually pays off, and the lines below are proof:

“Prada sweater, Valentinos (Valentinos)
You know when you get blessed, intimidate the ones who envy you
Hand sanitizer, I’m clean, I keep cash and it’s dirty (Cash)
I’m from where you can’t come and be seen, and it’s dirty (Woo, woo)”

He is aware that his success is the only thing that will keep his haters at bay. Lil Durk’s verses also show that he works hard to drive financial superiority, which he masks with his designer clothes. Lil Durk reminds everyone that he still comes from the hood, where the game is dirty, implying that he has worked tirelessly to be where he is today.

Khaled’s Message in Every Chance I Get

The song plays towards the message that DJ Khaled has been preaching since the beginning of his career that hard work pays. His musical success is proof of that. He also believes that every opportunity provides a chance to make moves towards that success, and Every Chance I Get is a summary of that message.

Khaled’s message in the Every Chance I Get song is straightforward; hustle hard and be patient. Eventually, the hustling will pay off, and when that happens, cherish your success and keep working hard.

Conclusion

The Every Chance I Get song is an anthem for success. DJ Khaled, Lil Baby, and Lil Durk all share common beliefs; working hard pays off and keeping one’s head up and focused towards the goal is critical to success. The powerful beats and inspiring lyrics should motivate everyone who is looking to achieve something in life.

The message in this incredible song is that every chance counts, and the opportunity to chase one’s dreams is available to everyone. With hard work and determination, anyone can achieve their goals.
