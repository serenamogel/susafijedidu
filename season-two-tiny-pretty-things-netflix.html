Will there be a season two of Tiny Pretty Things on Netflix?

TINY Pretty Things got viewers hooked - as the mystery of who pushed Cassie was slowly revealed.

And fans who have raced through the first series have been left wondering whether the show will be renewed for a second season.

Will there be a season 2 of Tiny Pretty Things?

So far, Netflix has not revealed any plans to renew the series for a second season on the platform.

But worry not, Netflix often waits a while after the debut of a series to decide whether it will be renewed.

The decision will be based on viewing figures and the popularity of the series.

The bad news is that the next series won't be out for at least a year.

Production began on the first season in August 2019 and wrapped in December, before being released a year later, so the same sort of timeframe could apply to a new series.

What could happen in season 2?

Of course, season two couldn't continue without unravelling the "death" of Ramon at the end of series one.

We don't know if Ramon is in fact dead, but if he is we can expect another whodunnit to dominate season two's storyline.

Luckily, there's plenty of source material left for producers to go off.

The book the series is based on has a sequel - called Shiny Broken Pieces by Sona Charaipotra and Dhonielle Clayton. 

The sequel picks up right where the first one ends.

In book two, Bette, June, and Neveah are all now seniors at the ballet school.

But the competition and backstabbing doesn't end after the school's showcase.

The girls must now fight to secure a spot at a esteemed ballet company after graduation - so we can expect there to be no shortage of drama!

How does season one end?

Season one ends with more mysteries than it began with.

Season one was dominated by the mystery about who pushed Cassie Shore - and viewers desperately watched to find out who it could be.

Most viewers suspected jealous Bette as behind the incident.

But in a shock twist, the culprit turned out to be Delia, Bette's older sister.

She pushed Cassie from the building when she discovered that Cassie had been sleeping with her boyfriend.
