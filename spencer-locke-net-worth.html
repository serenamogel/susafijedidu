Spencer Locke Net Worth

Spencer Locke net worth:

$5 million

Spencer Locke is an American actress whose Hollywood career began in Kidz Bop: Everyone’s a Star!. Her first movie appearance was in Spanglish. She went on to have a recurring roles on the sitcoms Ned’s Declassified School Survival Guide and Phil of the Future. She then became known for her appearance in the film Resident Evil: Extinction and its sequel Resident Evil: Afterlife. In the 2010s, she had roles in the films Detention and Landmine Goes Click. She also played Jane Porter in 2013’s Tarzan and played the role of Kylie on the TV series Cougar Town.
