8 Things You Didnt Know About Geetika Tyagi

➡ Geetika Tyagi Biography

Geetika Tyagi is a well-known Indian actress and producer, who works in the Hindi film industry primarily. She has worked in TV serials, for example, Powder (2010) and Brings on the Night (2012). She started getting fame with the short film, The Eclipse Of Taregna (2011). Afterward, Geetika has acted in movies like Aatma (2013), What The Fish (2013), One by Two (2014), and Tera Mera Tedha Medha (2015). She turned into a producer too with the documentary, In Their Shoes in 2015. In 2017 Geetika starred in director Kranti Kanad CRD (2017) as well. Here we are telling you 8 amazing facts about this favorite celebrity of yours that you will be amazed to know. So let us take a start!

You will be surely amazed to know that this beautiful actress had a pure non-film background so she had to undergo a great deal of struggle in order to make her mark in this glamorous world of showbiz.
