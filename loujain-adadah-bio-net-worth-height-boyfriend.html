Get To Know Loujain Adadah Bio, Net Worth, Height, Boyfriend

Loujain Adadah was left with riches when her billionaire husband passed away in 2016. So, in hopes of continuing his legacy, while also making a mark of her own, the former model joined the cast of Dubai Bling.

Get to know her better as this Loujain Adadah Bio proceeds. 

Loujain Adadah On Dubai Bling

Netflix’s new show Dubai Bling put all other wealth to shame. Flaunting the daily lives of Dubai’s wealthiest socialite residents, the show let the viewers experience the beautiful city of Dubai through the eyes of 10 successful personalities including our star, Loujain Adadah.

A mother of two, Loujain was the newest arrival to the roaring Dubai social scene. She was “outspoken, assertive, and not shy to speak her mind.” And the only thing she was wishing for from the show was a “fresh start .”

“A new Chapter begins #dubaibling starts today on @netflixmena @netflix,” Loujain posted on her IG sharing a clip from the show.

Joining Loujain for the inaugural season of Dubai Bling were her co-stars — DJ Bliss, Farhana Bodi, Kris Fade, Brianna Fade, Zeina Khoury, Danya Mohammed,  Lojain Omran, Ebraheem Al Samadi, and Safa Siddiqui.

Loujain Adadah Net Worth

Loujain Adadah flaunted a net worth of above £50 million in 2022.

Coming from a humble background, Loujain received her early education in The States and then continued in Beirut. And while she was still at school, she did her first magazine advertisement shoot which grew her interest in photography. After graduation, the star then took a few courses in fashion design.

At 21, Loujain then got her first TV presenting gig to host a program about music on MTV Lebanon called Energy Spin Magazine. This was the turning point in her life and the start of her budding career.

Later, she was then offered to host another TV reality program which she couldn’t continue because she got married.

Her biggest dream now is to be either a TV producer or an actress and if it were in music she wants to learn how to DJ professionally.

Fun Fact: Loujain is a philanthropist and believes that philanthropy is not about money. It’s about feeling the pain of others and caring enough about their needs to help.

Loujain Adadah Boyfriend

Loujain Adadah didn’t talk about her dating life after her husband’s death.

Her late husband, Dr. Walid Juffali passed away from lung cancer in July 2016, at the age of 61. He was one of the wealthiest business families in Saudi Arabia. Educated at Le Rosey in Switzerland, Walid went on to become the chairman of his family’s company, E.A. Juffali Brothers — one of the largest companies in Saudi Arabia for telecommunications, infrastructure, and insurance.

After Walid passed away, Loujain grieved for years. Given that she was fairly young and just a new mother, she cried every night realizing that their dream would never come true. But now, instead of tears, she smiles when she thinks of him. “Me and my daughters’ loss left a hole in our hearts and I know it will never go away, we never truly get over a loss but we move forward and evolve from it,” she explained.

As for their kids, the duo shares two, Talia and Lana, who turned 8 and 6 on October 30, and May 28, 2022, respectively.

The Dubai Bling star still has pictures of her first husband on her IG.

But though Walid was Loujain’s first spouse, she wasn’t his first, nor his second! Walid was married three times. Firstly, to Basma al-Sulaiman, with whom he bears three children. Secondly, to the American model, Christina Estrada, in Dubai in 2001, with whom he had a daughter. And finally, he married Loujain in 2012 in a remarkable ceremony in Venice reported to have cost £10 million

It was also said that Loujain’s wedding sparked a divorce from her husband’s ex-wife Christina. Reportedly, from the £200 million divorce battle, Christina was awarded the biggest divorce settlement in English history of £75 million.
