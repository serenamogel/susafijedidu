Jeffrey Dean Morgan is an American actor, producer, and voiceover artist who is known for his roles like Negan in the post-apocalyptic horror drama series The Walking Dead, Dr. Edward Marcase in the UPN science fiction drama series The Burning Zone, Denny Duquette in the ABC medical drama series Grey’s Anatomy, Edward Blake/Comedian in the superhero film Watchmen, Joe DiMaggio in the made-for-TV drama miniseries The Secret Life of Marilyn Monroe, John Winchester/Azazel in The WB dark fantasy horror drama series Supernatural, William Gallagher in the romantic drama film P.S. I Love You, Jason Crouse in the legal and political drama series The Good Wife, JD Richter in the sci-fi drama series Extant, Agent Harvey Russell in the science fiction monster film Rampage, and Max in the thriller film The Resident.
