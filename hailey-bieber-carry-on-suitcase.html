Shop Hailey Bieber's Favorite Carry-On Travel Suitcase

In: stylish luggage that’s actually beneficial for travel. Out: dirty old luggage that you’ve used for ten years and has one wheel that doesn’t work. Okay, so that wheel part may be coming from personal experience, but luggage is one of those splurge buys that is more of a long-term investment (that you — especially frequent travelers — will never regret!). Hailey Bieber is just one of many celebrities who fell for the Béis craze — the travel accessory and luggage brand founded by none other than Pretty Little Liars actress Shay Mitchell.

Back in January 2023, Bieber wrote in a non-sponsored Instagram story, “I love Béis, it’s the best suitcase in the world [and] the only one I travel with, it has the smoothest wheels, it’s functional and oh so chic.”

Beis The Carry-On Roller in Grey

Beis The Carry-On Roller in Grey

The model was pictured with the company’s $218 Carry-On Roller in grey. The carry-on comes in six other colors with a cushioned trolley handle, TSA locks, a weight limit indicator, and more thoughtful travel benefits. Our favorite aspect of the bag is probably the separate pouch meant for dirty clothes and damp clothing. There is nothing worse than having to mix in a wet bathing suit with your clean clothes!

If you’re looking for more than just a carry-on, Béis also sells the 26” Check-In Roller bag for (you guessed it) checked bags. This suitcase runs at a slightly higher price at $308 a pop. The brand often has limited edition color drops for returning customer favorites and new collabs.

Aside from Hailey Bieber’s stellar review, Béis customers seem to enjoy the carry-on suitcase as well. One buyer wrote, “This is the perfect carry-on — I never have an issue in any overhead compartment, plus it makes it super easy to stay organized. It's perfect for all trips, big or small.” Another commented on “the combination of functionality, design, and beauty.” One warning for the overpackers out there, though. With its spacious compartments and pockets, the bag can get a bit heavier than you’d think (holding anywhere from 49 to 61 pounds). Coming from someone with less-than-ideal arm strength, make sure you're packing smarter, not harder!

Jessica Alba, Meghan Trainor, and Ariana Madix are just a few other big names who have been seen sporting Béis. In fact, Los Angeles Rams player Puka Nacua recently rolled Béis into an NFL stadium. Yes, even athletes can have aesthetic luggage that color coordinates with their outfits, too.
