Dave Grohl Net Worth

Dave Grohl is an American rock musician born on January 14, 1969 in Warren, OH. He is best known for his work as the drummer of the iconic grunge band Nirvana and as the founder and lead singer of the Foo Fighters. Grohl has been a major influence in the rock music scene since the early 1990s and continues to be a major force in the industry today.
