Saysh One Sneaker by Allyson Felix

When Oprah says, "Jump!" we say, "How high?", and if she said, "Now you get a car!" to us, well, we'd jump through the roof. Unfortunately, The Oprah Winfrey Show isn't around anymore, and neither are its memorable giveaways. But at least we have her Favorite Things list. It's no free car, but it's something,

There are so many incredible pieces that made the cut this year, from these weatherproof boots by Tory Burch and a cozy Ugg teddy bear coat to go with them to two pairs of her go-to slides for around the house. Now, we've got our eyes on Oprah's favorite sneaker, which is designed by the most decorated American track-and-field Olympian, Allyson Felix.

Shop now: $120; amazon.com

The Saysh One sneaker is lightweight and extremely comfortable to walk or run in, and you don't have to be an Olympic athlete (or a celebrity) to appreciate it. Plus, Felix ran in these sneakers during the the Tokyo Olympics and took home an individual bronze. If that fact and Oprah's seal of approval aren't enough to convince you to add to cart immediately, then we're not sure what will.
