Damon Wayans Net Worth

Damon Wayans is an American actor, comedian, and writer born on September 4, 1960 in New York City, NY. He is best known for his role as Michael Richard Kyle, Sr. on ABC's My Wife and Kids from 2001 to 2005, as well as his work on the sketch comedy show In Living Color. He has also starred in several films, including Major Payne, Celtic Pride, and Bulletproof. Wayans is part of a show-biz family, and has often shared the screen with his siblings Marlon, Shawn, Keenen, and Kim.
