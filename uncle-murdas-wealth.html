Leonard Grant’s Biography

Born on July 25, 1980, in Brooklyn, New York City, Leonard Grant, known by his stage name Uncle Murda or U.M., is a prominent hip hop artist. He gained recognition through his studio album, eight mixtapes, and hit singles such as “Bullet Bullet”, “Run The City”, and “Murdera”. He has been actively involved in the music industry since 1999.

Uncle Murda’s net worth is estimated to be over $500,000 as of mid-2016, earned through his successful career in the music industry as a hip hop artist, collaborating with several renowned musicians.

Uncle Murda’s Net Worth $500,000

Little is known about Uncle Murda’s early life before his active career in the late 1990s. However, his breakthrough came in the second half of the 2000s when DJ Green Lantern, his agent, introduced his music to Jay-Z, leading to a signing with Roc-A-Fella Records. Although brief, his time with the label, during which he released the mixtape “Murda’sMuzik” in 2007, was pivotal in shaping his net worth.

Since then, he has released nine successful mixtapes, including “Hard To Kill” (2008), “Rudy King” (2008), and “Yellow Tape” (2016), significantly contributing to the growth of his net worth.

Uncle Murda’s collaborations with notable rap artists like Busta Rhymes, French Montana, and 50 Cent have also added to his wealth. Moreover, his involvement in the Urban Wrestling Federation in 2011 further increased his net worth.

Little is known about Uncle Murda’s personal life, as he prefers to keep it private. In 2008, he survived a headshot, refusing to cooperate with the police investigation. He maintains an active presence on social media platforms such as Twitter and Instagram.
