Cyntoia Brown Wiki, Age, Boyfriend, Biography, Family, Height &amp; More

Cyntoia Brown (born in 1988) is an American Criminal and Controversial Figure from Nashville, Tennessee. In 2004, she came into the headlines of the news when Brown murdered Johnny Allen (a 43-year-old real estate agent). Furthermore, the 28-year-old Cyntoia confessed she killed Allen in the attempt of self-defense during her trial.

But the prosecutor claimed that Johnny was sleeping when he shot dead. Later, Brown sentenced to 51 years to life for first-degree murder. Moreover, she is serving her time in the Tennessee Prison for Women in Nashville, Tennessee.
