Luke Taylor is famous as a celebrity kid. He is the son of Zac Taylor.

Dream turned reality

Zac‘s dream of becoming an NFL head coach turned into reality after being appointed as a tenth head coach of Cincinnati Bengals in 2019. 

Before that, he served at  Miami Dolphins and Los Angeles Rams as an assistant quarterback coach. Thirty-five years old Zac becomes the second young head coach in NFL. 

Zac will be returning in the third season despite going 6-25-1 in his past two seasons. 

The team president Mike Brown stated remaining bullish on the foundation Zac is building and looking forward to winning results while announcing his return on 4th January 2021.

Married to the love of his life

Zac is happily married to the love of his life, Sarah Sherman Taylor. The couple first met while studying at Nebraska University.  

At the time, Sarah was pursuing a master’s degree specialized in marketing and journalism. 

She was associated with Nebraska‘s sports information department as a graduate assistant. Zac started running onto Sarah more often after realizing her age is 22, which he initially thought 30. 

He thought he had a chance. His humble and kind nature attracted her, and they eventually started dating. 

Within a month of dating, Zac realized Sarah is the one with whom he can be for the rest of his life. The couple got married in 2008.

Football themed wedding ceremony

The couple organized the football theme wedding ceremony at St. Annes Catholic Church, officiated by the team Chaplain. 

The long lists of guests included half of the Houston Texans staff. They cut the cake shaped in football. The wedding was followed by a reception at River Oak Country Club.
